Computer Museums in Netherlands

The are many museums in the Netherlands that offer a great experience for nerds and families; not always for both groups at the same time, but that's you to choose. Those museums are difficult to find by casual tourists and even local inhabitants.

National Videogame Museum

https://www.nationaalvideogamemuseum.nl/

 

Home Computer Museum

https://www.homecomputermuseum.nl/

 

Bonami Computer Museum

https://computermuseum.nl/

 

Computer Museum - University of Amsterdam

https://ub.fnwi.uva.nl/computermuseum/

The collection will be shown and demonstrated on written request.
